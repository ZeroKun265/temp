Careful:

The exported PDF will not be exceptionally pretty, be advised!

Make also sure to avoid using numbers and special characters in the text, as the translation for those is not standardized and may be different depending on the font/references

Also note that uppercase letters and lowercase letters appear identical

Also also, there is no equivalent of the letter C in dragon language, normally you'd use K or S depending on context, here we just use the letter K as a replacement
